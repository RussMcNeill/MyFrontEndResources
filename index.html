Bellevue/East Side Traffic

I-405 is the major north-south arterial for Bellevue and the surrounding east side (of Lake Washington) communities.

Highway 520 is a an east-west highway that crosses Lake Washington to Seattle and extends to the east into Redmond

I-90 crosses Lake Washington south of 520, and extends east to Issaquah (and beyond, all the way across the country).

All of the above highways have high occupancy vehicle (HOV) lanes. In addition, I-405 north of Bellevue and 520 across Lake Washington have Express Toll lanes. These are HOV lanes that allow single occupancy vehicles to buy access with a "Good to Go" pass.

Traffic is worst during morning and afternoon rush hour.
